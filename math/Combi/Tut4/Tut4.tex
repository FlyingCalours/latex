\paragraph{Q11} Find the coefficient of $w^2x^2y^2z^2$ in the expansion of

\medskip \noindent
(a) $(w + x + y + z + 1)^{10}$

\medskip \noindent \textit{Solution.} 

\noindent
The coefficient of $w^2x^2y^2z^2$ in the given expression after expansion is 
\[\binom{10}{2,2,2,2,2} = \frac{10!}{2!2!2!2!2!}=113400.\]
\hfill $\square$

\noindent
(b) $(2w-x+3y+z-2)^{12}$

\noindent
Similarly, the coefficient in the given expression after expansion is
\begin{align*}
\binom{12}{2,2,2,2,(12-2-2-2-2)}
    \cdot2^2
    \cdot(-1)^2
    \cdot3^2
    \cdot1^2
    \cdot(-2)^{(12-2-2-2-2)}
&=\frac{12!}{2!2!2!2!4!}
\cdot2^2
\cdot(-1)^2
\cdot3^2
\cdot1^718502400
\cdot(-2)^4\\
&=718502400.
\end{align*}
\hfill $\square$
